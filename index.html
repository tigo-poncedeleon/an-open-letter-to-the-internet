A Letter to the Internet

Dear Internet,

Oh endless thread,
Where memes and dreams are daily spread.
A link, a click, and off we go,
To cat vids, hacks, and things to know.
You’re chaos wrapped in fiber’s glow,
Both wise and weird, high-tech tableau.
Click here, scroll there,
You’re everywhere, yet made of air.
Oh Internet, our quirky guide,
We love your heart, forgive your pride.

What I’d do without you,
Only heavens knows,
For you shine so bright,
In your interconnected network flow,
Please don’t crumble under AI,
And create a hellish dystopia,
For this life.

All my love I send to you,
From your very good friend,
Mr. Tigo bids adieu.
